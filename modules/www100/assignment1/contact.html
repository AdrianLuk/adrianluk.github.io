Driving directions

If you are heading EAST on Lake Shore Boulevard:

  1. Turn right onto Lower Simcoe Street
  2. Head South to Coding Court

If you are heading WEST:

  1. Take Queens Quay West
  2. Turn left into our site at Lower Simcoe Street.

  • Please note the new left turn signal light.
  • Also note that eastbound traffic on Queens Quay West is not permitted to turn right into our site.
  • Underground parking is available on-site at 555 Coding Court, or above-ground one block west at Rees Street and Queens Quay West.
