Warm Colors

Red

Red is typically associated with power and importance. It is by far the most stimulating color, typically being used for warnings and prompts for action due to it increasing breathing and pulse. It also symbolizes passion and romance which is why it is used in fashion brands and also in dating websites.

Orange

Orange is a "fun" color that can evoke a sense of urgency, energy, or impulse. It is typically used sparingly, usually only used to bring attention to something. For example, Amazon uses orange to bring attention to their "limited time offer" banner. However, orange is sometimes interpreted as "cheap." Thus if the product is cheap or wants to be seen as cheap, then orange is a good choice.

Yellow

Yellow is a complicated color. Depending on the shade used, yellow can be very energetic, or also give off a sense of antiquity. Bright yellow is most energetic, but does not imply as much severity as red. Middle or neutral shades of yellow give off a feeling of comfort while still being energetic. Dark shades can give the impression of antiquity, wisdom, and curiosity.

Cool Colors

Blue

Blue is one of the most common colors. Blue gives off a feeling of trustworthiness, security, loyalty, peace, and order. Facebook and many banks use the color blue intentionally to give their customers the subconscious feeling that they are able to be trusted with sensitive information. There is a common consensus that blue should not be used for anything related to food as it is believed to suppress appetites.

Green

Green is used generally for environmental and outdoor products. Naturally, it is connected to nature, eco-friendliness, and the environment. If your product has anything to do with the outdoors or the environment, you can never go wrong with the color green. Lighter shades of green can also be associated with innovation and creativity.

Purple

Historically, purple was associated with royalty. So it is no surprise that purple is still connected to luxury, lavishness and wealth. It is most commonly used for fashion and luxury goods. It also exudes elegance and femininity and spans a large female demographic. Purple is also a color with no negative associations.

Neutral Colors

Black

Black is a color which promotes classiness, sophistication, and conventional. It suggests excellence and formality as well as luxury and exclusivity. Black is closely associated with timelessness and is used a lot in high-end products.

Grey

Grey is a color which promotes neutrality and formality. Depending on the shade of grey used, emotions associated can range from sad and somber, to modern and contemporary. It is very useful for backgrounds due to it's neutral properties and varying shades to accomplish different objectives.

White

White is an often overlooked color, probably because it is seen as a lack of color. It is most associated with purity and innocence as well as simplicity and spaciousness. Many well designed websites make very good use of white space to accent other colors, give a sense of freedom, and even give off a modern vibe.
