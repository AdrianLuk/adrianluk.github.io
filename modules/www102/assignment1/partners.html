ARC EDUCATION CENTRE PARTNERS

Benedict Chan

Director of Music

B. Mus Ed, Art Dip

Benedict completed his B.Mus in Music Education and Artist Diploma in Performance at the University of Western Ontario. With trumpet being his primary instrument, he is equally at home with guitar, bass, clarinet and theory. Benedict has studied trumpet under Scott Harrison in the lineage of Vincent Chichowicz, and Shawn Spicer. He has been teaching instruments and theory for many years. Benedict is actively teaching privately at Arc as well as in Via Musica in Mississauga. Benedict is also an accomplished audio-visual specialist, with years of experiences in production of various events from small venue gatherings, to large scale conferences.

Vicky Li

Director of Art

BFA

Vicky is a graduate of OCAD University majoring in Criticism and Curatorial Practice. Throughout her arts education, she focused heavily on developing her curatorial practice as well as her artistic practice. She is educated in drawing and painting as an artistic focus during most of her life. In school she branched out to learn techniques such as mould-making, printmaking, and ceramics in order to have a greater understanding of the artwork she curates. She believes in having knowledge of the medium and techniques of the artworks. She wishes to feature in order to do the artist and their work justice.

Helen To

Director of Education

B.A (RECE) B. ED (OCT)

Helen is a Registered Early Childhood Educator (B.A.) and an Ontario Certified Teacher (B.ED) from Ryerson University and York University. Helen is an enthusiastic individual that is passionate for learning and teaching in an inclusive space, committed to the well-being of students. She has a passion for the Arts and Mathematics where she integrates cross-curriculuar content throughout her teaching. She believes that students are capable, independent, and curious learners; therefore, she incorporates contemporary strategies in teaching by building on strengths and supporting their interests. Helen's goal is to inspire students to reach their dreams and to help them build bridges in learning under our Arc.
