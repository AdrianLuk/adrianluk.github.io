Who are we?

The League of Heroes is a collection of some of Earth’s mightiest costumed super-heroes! We’ve decided to use our powers for the forces of good to fight evil in all its forms. We believe in changing the world, and we believe the best way to do that is in spandex and a cape! The League is the world's most powerful collection of crime fighters.
